Available Diploma Thesis Projects

This is an indicative list of proposed topics for diploma theses that fall under the current interests of the Group and its senior members that will be able to oversee and advice students who choose them. Each topic includes a description and supervisor contact details. Students are also encouraged to go through this list and contact respected supervisors if they are interested in subjects and topics that may not be listed here but fall under the general themes that are described. Note that it is expected that a diploma thesis is equivalent with a full time work of a semester (or more accurately a roughly six month period).
